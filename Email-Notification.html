['Microsoft June 2024 Patch Tuesday fixes 51 flaws, 18 RCEs']

Microsoft have released their monthly Patch Tuesday cycle for June. This has been assessed by the AUCloud Security Operations Team. Of note, there were 51 Vulnerabilities patched this month, with (1) of these assessed as CRITICAL, 50 assessed as IMPORTANT. There were Zero Zero-Days vulnerabilities.
